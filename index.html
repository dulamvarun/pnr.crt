About PNR

PNR Clean Room Technologies is a leading manufacturer and supplier of advanced clean room and cool room solutions, based in Jeedimetla, Hyderabad. We specialize in designing and delivering controlled environments for pharmaceutical, biotechnology, electronics, and research laboratory applications.

Our expertise spans modular panels, air handling units, air showers, pass boxes, and specialized lab equipment. With a commitment to timely delivery, quality products, and comprehensive solutions, PNR is a trusted partner for industries requiring strict environmental standards and operational excellence.
